how mining works?

  1. Mining Session:
    Click “Start Mining” once per day. Runs until the next reset at 12:00 UTC.
  2. Earning Rate:
    You earn 0.1 $BLACK/sec × your Mining Power (boosted by codes).
  3. Your Daily Code:
    A new 10-character code is generated each day at 12:00 UTC.
  4. Code Submissions:
    Friends submit your code, you gain +0.1× Mining Power per submission (max 10/day), and they get +0.5×. Resets at 12:00 UTC.
  5. Referrals:
    New users who join via your link count as referrals you earn a token bonus per referral.
  6. Daily Reset:
    At 12:00 UTC every day, all mining sessions stop, codes regenerate.
